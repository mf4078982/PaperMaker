About Online Paper Maker

Online Paper Maker is a simple yet powerful web application designed to help teachers, students, and institutes prepare professional-quality test papers in minutes. Our mission is to make assessment creation easier, faster, and more reliable.

Our Mission

To empower educators worldwide by providing easy-to-use tools for test preparation, online exams, and answer sheet evaluation without technical hurdles.

Key Features

  • Convert images of questions into PDF test papers
  • Read answer sheets using device camera
  • Publish secure online exams with time limits
  • Crop questions and remove unnecessary content
  • Multiple subscription plans (Free, Pro, Enterprise)

Our Team

We are a team of developers and educators passionate about digital learning. Our goal is to simplify teaching and examination processes for everyone.
